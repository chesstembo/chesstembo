Welcome to Tembo Chess

Welcome to Tembo Chess ("the App"), developed by Tembo Chess / Tembo inc ("we", "our", or "us"). Your privacy is important, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information. By using Tembo Chess, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum data necessary for the App to function.

1.1 Account Information (Optional)

If you choose to sign in using Firebase Authentication, we may collect:

  • Email address

This is used ONLY for:

  • Saving your training progress
  • Ranking you on leaderboards
  • Preventing duplicate accounts

1.2 Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data such as:

  • Puzzle completion statistics
  • App performance metrics
  • Device type
  • General analytics (non-identifiable)

1.3 Local Storage (On Your Device Only)

Tembo Chess stores the following locally on your device, NOT on our servers:

  • Downloaded puzzle packs
  • User puzzle progress
  • Settings and preferences

We do not collect or upload this data.

2. Advertising & Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Tembo Chess uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • App interactions
  • Approximate location (not precise)

This data is used solely for:

  • Ad delivery and personalization
  • Fraud prevention
  • Analytics and performance measurement

Opt-out: Users may opt out of personalized ads using Google device settings or through AdMob's consent management platform.

2.1 Firebase Services

We use Google Firebase for essential app functionality:

  • Firebase Authentication - Secure user login
  • Firebase Firestore - Data storage
  • Firebase Analytics - App performance insights
  • Firebase Crashlytics - Error reporting

SDK Compliance: All Firebase SDKs are Google Play SDK-certified and comply with Google Play User Data Policy.

We use collected information for:
